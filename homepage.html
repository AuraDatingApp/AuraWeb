Safe date protocol

Clear steps that keep every encounter private, verified, and under your control.

With our safety protocol, you can choose between a regular date or a tracked date, depending on your comfort level.
During a tracked date, you decide what information you want to share with our servers — maintaining control and privacy at all times.
Stay safe by sharing your real-time location and choosing from our network of verified safe environments in your city for first encounters.
Upon arrival, confirm that your date is verified and registered with us through real-time personal verification.
Use your customizable panic button to instantly alert trusted contacts in case of discomfort or emergency.
Generate a real-time date report to share with authorities if needed, ensuring immediate assistance and accountability.
Set a return-time schedule — if you don’t return before the scheduled hour, the app automatically notifies your chosen contacts.
Update the schedule anytime in case your date goes well or plans change.
Keep the app active and updated during your date — that’s how we ensure full protection and peace of mind.
